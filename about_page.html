  • Welcome!

  • DosiiRoc Cafe was founded in late 2023 by chef and owner June Park. While living in Rochester for over 30 years, June opened her home to countless loved ones- including friends, homesick international students, and her colleagues from work. June's passion for hosting and serving food to her loved ones inspired her to venture into the restaurant business with the support of her daughter, Sara. DosiiRoc Cafe strives to blend authentic Korean food and cafe culture into one space by offering both traditional and authentic dishes with trendy and handcrafted beverages that blend typical American cafe offerings with flavors inspired by Korean culture. We hope you can come enjoy a cozy, home-style meal in our space sometime soon.

  • With Love, June and Sara Park
